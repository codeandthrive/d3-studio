# Project 01 - Reframing Vaayanasala as a Public Realm

## Type
Master's Studio - Deakin University

## Overview
Vaayanasala, traditionally known as a reading room, historically functioned as a socio-cultural hub. The project repositions it as a contemporary public realm by integrating hospitality, art, workspaces, and communal programs within a cohesive architectural framework.

## Concept Focus
"Existing system... but no architectural intervention?"

The proposal formalizes existing community patterns while retaining cultural continuity.

## Key Strategies
- Revive socio-cultural identity.
- Expand Vaayanasala into a public realm.
- Configure spaces through hierarchy of courtyards and streets.
- Define semi-public and semi-private zoning.
- Enable large gatherings, political events, and temporary public health functions.
