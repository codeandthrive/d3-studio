# Project 02 - Flinders Street Railway Station Proposal

## Type
Studio Project - Deakin University

## Overview
Flinders Street Station is an iconic landmark of Melbourne. The proposal restructures pedestrian movement and reconnects the urban river edge to the heritage station while preserving the civic character of the precinct.

## Design Direction
The scheme restores links to the Yarra River and frames movement through public plazas, stepped edges, and walkable corridors.

## Core Strategies
- Connect to Yarra River through transition corridor design.
- Improve pedestrian connectivity from bridge and square networks.
- Respect heritage skyline and station identity.
- Connect ecological corridors along the river edge.
- Create a public waterfront interface.
