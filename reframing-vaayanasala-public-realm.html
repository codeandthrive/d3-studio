Reframing Vaayanasala as a Public Realm

Master's Studio - Deakin University

Vaayanasala, traditionally known as a reading room, is repositioned as a contemporary public realm by integrating hospitality, art, workspace, and communal programs within a cohesive architectural framework.

The intervention formalizes existing community patterns while retaining socio-cultural continuity.

Strategies
  • Revive socio-cultural identity through craft and performance-linked spaces.
  • Expand Vaayanasala as a public realm.
  • Configure hierarchy of courtyards and streets.
  • Define semi-public and semi-private zoning.
  • Support large gatherings and event-based public use.
